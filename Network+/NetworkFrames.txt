Basics of Network Frames

Network Interfacing Card (NIC)
	A NIC is plugged into pc which is connected to a network switch.
How is Data sent over a network cable?
	Sent in binary form. Data is sent in frames, or sometimes refered to as packets. Packetized data. 
How are frames formatted?
	A frame can be up to 1500 bytes long, with a descrete beginning and end. Frames are generated inside Network card, then 
sends through network.
